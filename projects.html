Locarno Film Festival

The Locarno Film Festival is an annual event taking place every August in Locarno, Switzerland. Here is my proposal for the 2021 edition of the festival. This poster serves as a tribute to Ennio Morricone, who passed away that year. The depicted figure in outline represents him, while the tiger prints draw inspiration from the festival itself.
